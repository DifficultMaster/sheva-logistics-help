Here is a list of most popular questions with answers.

 

 

Q: Why should I join Sheva Logistics?

A: With over 20.000 Virtual Trucking Companies across the TruckersMP network players do want to make sure they're joining a quality VTC. Sheva Logistics is one of ~100 validated VTCs around TruckersMP network with public and private convoys hosted twice a week across both DLC and non-DLC areas, friendly drivers, a big variety of driver ranks, loyal staff members and one of the greatest trucking experiences you'll find around!

-------------------------------------

Q: How to join Sheva Logistics?

A: Please, fullfill our application form over at https://www.shevalogistics.com/apply and our staff will soon contact you by Discord.

-------------------------------------

Q: How to setup my paintjob combo, player tag and an automatic delivery logging service app?

A: Please, visit the  #setup-info  channel for more details.

-------------------------------------

Q: What does validated status do to a VTC?

A: Having such status means that this VTC was checked by TruckersMP Staff Members for quality. Having such priority means that our VTC has more possibilities available for it's members.

-------------------------------------

Q: Which games does Sheva Logistics support?

A: We support deliveries and host convoys in both Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator.

-------------------------------------

Q: How often do you host convoys?

A: We usually do host convoys on schedule, each other Thursday and every Sunday with departure time of 17:00 UTC or 18:00 UTC. We publish information about our next convoys on our social media channels.

-------------------------------------

Q: How do I know when there's a VTC event?

A: All of the public event information is published in #public-events  

-------------------------------------

Q: Do I have to attend every convoy?

A: You can attend our public convoys once a month for the completion of your monthly distance requirement, but it is not required to attend all of our convoys.

-------------------------------------

Q: Do I need to always use our combo and player tag?

A: The use of player tag is always required, but you only have to use our paintjob combo during our events or the events that Sheva Logistics is attending to.

-------------------------------------

Q: What is an automatic delivery logging and do you have it?

A: Automatic delivery logging services allow players to throw away the routine of manually fullfilling the delivery logs - it is done automatically once the required app is installed. And yes, we DO have automatic delivery logging service supported.

-------------------------------------

Q: Do you allow the use of double and triple trailers?

A: Only in-game double and triple trailers are allowed. For Euro Truck Simulator 2, those would be Double, B-Double and HCT trailers, while for American Truck Simulator - various double and triple trailer combinations.
