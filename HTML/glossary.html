Here is a list of all website-specific definitions.

 Sheva Logistics carries out an ultimate goal of building an amazing community of virtual truck sim enjoyers. The company was created by a pair of virtual trucking enthusiasts on July 2020 and has massively progressed since then. We're striving to provide the best conditions for our drivers possible, all the while making sure they get the most stunning virtual trucking experience under unusually low requirements, allowing for almost everyone to experience Sheva and taking huge amounts of stress off our drivers.

 

 

А

 

 About – website page that has detailed information on Sheva Logistics, such as it’s history, offers, and statistics.

 

 Applicant – a physical person filling an application.

 

 Application – filled form, sent by applicants and to be reviewed by staff to be either accepted, or declined.

 

 Apply – action, associated with applying to Sheva Logistics or it’s staff teams, usually transfers either to application instructions page, or directly to application form that follows otherwise.

 

 Automatic Delivery Logging – feature, associated with automated logging of the VTC drivers’ deliveries, with detailed information present for each. In Sheva Logistics, we use TrucksBook and it’s services for automatic delivery logging.

 

C

 

 Community centre – the heart of Sheva Logistics community, it’s Discord server.

 

 Community members – members of Sheva Logistics community, normally those, present within the Sheva Logistics community centre.

 

 Contact – action, associated with sending feedback to be reviewed by staff, usually transfers to feedback form.

 

 Convoys – events, both public and private, where a controlled column of participants drives from set start to finish.

 

D

 

 Discord server – server hosted on social network called Discord, for Sheva Logistics it is a main community centre.

 

 Driver of the Month – competition hosted by Sheva Logistics, where on a monthly basis the most active driver within the VTC is determined by both the delivery distance they have covered and the VTC events that they have attended.

 

 Driver ranking – feature, associated with an hierarchy of VTC driver ranks, each with it’s own benefits, based on their level of experience and activity within Sheva Logistics.

 

G

 

 Gallery – website page that contains both pictures selected for public showcase by staff, and the winning works of the monthly Screenshot of the Month competitions.

 

M

 

 Meet our team – website page that contains a full list of all current Sheva Logistics members – both drivers and staff and their according ranks and/or positions.

 

N

 

 Newswire – website page that contains a full list of all currently relevant public news Sheva Logistics has put out on other platforms – both public events and global announcements.

 

P

 

 Paintjob combo – a colour scheme and a design that Sheva Logistics members must wear on their company vehicles in-game.

 

 Privacy Policy – website page that contains full privacy policy and terms of service agreements, as well as GDPR-related statements concerning user data privacy.

 

S

 

 Screenshot of the Month – competition hosted by Sheva Logistics, where on a monthly basis the most favoured by Sheva Logistics community in-game picture work is determined and put on public display indefinitely.

 

 SHL – short abbreviature for “Sheva Logistics”.

 

 Sheva Logistics – a virtual trucking company, on whose website you are currently present.

 

 Staff – members of Sheva Logistics, that are responsible for managing and/or working in it’s various branches.

 

 Staff Recruitment – feature, associated with a system that allows public to apply for Sheva Logistics staff.

 

T

 

 Tag – a constant text string, that Sheva Logistics members must wear as their tag on online in-game platforms, such as TruckersMP.

 

 Terms of Service – website page that contains full privacy policy and terms of service agreements, as well as GDPR-related statements concerning user data privacy.

 

 TruckersMP – an unofficial online in-game platform which Sheva Logistics is mainly based on.

 

V

 

 VTC , Virtual trucking company – a community of people, usually based on Euro Truck Simulator and/or American Truck Simulator franchise games.

 

 VTC Drivers – drivers, that belong to a virtual trucking company and as such, usually have a specific set of rules and benefits applied to them.

 

W

 

 Welcome – website’s home page, that has the general information about Sheva Logistics, and quick getaways to other pages.
